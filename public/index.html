Why Hangoutio?

Planning a meeting with your friends can be very difficult. Everyone has work, responsibilities, and a full schedule.

Hangoutio streamlines the process by making sharing ideas, personal availability, and much more extremely simple.

Intuitive

Our goal is to make planning a hangout an easy task for everyone.

Customizable

You're in control. It's easy to tailor all the aspects of a hangout to your needs when creating it, then edit it later if needed.

Free

Hangoutio is completely free for everyone, and can be used without registering an account.

Join Hangoutio

You don't need an account to plan a hangout, but having one grants you a few benefits:

  • Access multiple hangouts with the same account.
  • View all your previous and upcoming hangouts.
  • Set customizable email notification for your hangouts.
  • Add friends to further simplify the process.

Creating an account is completely free, and you can delete it at any time if you change your mind.
