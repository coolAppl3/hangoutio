Why Hangoutio?

Planning a meeting with your friends can be very difficult. Everyone has jobs, responsibilities, and a full schedule.

Hangoutio streamlines planning, making idea sharing, availability tracking, and catch-ups effortless with just a few clicks.

Get started Take a tour

Intuitive

In alignment with our goal to make planning a hangout a simple task for everyone, we made Hangoutio very easy to use.

Customizable

You have many options to configure things like budget, due date, voting period, and much more to suit your meetup needs.

Free

Hangoutio is free for everyone and an account is not needed for planning a hangout.

Join Hangoutio.

You don't need an account to plan a hangout, but having one grants you a few benefits:

  • Track all your previous and incoming hangouts.
  • Set email reminders and notifications.
  • Add friends to further simplify the process.

Creating an account is completely free, and you can delete it at any time if you change your mind.
